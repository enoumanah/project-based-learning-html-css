Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles, half of the ricotta mixture, half of the meat, and a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers, finishing with noodles, marinara sauce, and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
