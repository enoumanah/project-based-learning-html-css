Burger

Description

Burgers are a classic fast food item that can be customized with various toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 burger patty
  • 1 burger bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese slice (optional)
  • Ketchup and mustard to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the burger patty on a grill or stovetop until fully cooked.
  2. Toast the burger bun lightly.
  3. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun.
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, cheese, and any other desired toppings.
  5. Spread ketchup and mustard on the top bun and place it on the burger.
  6. Serve hot with fries or a side salad.
