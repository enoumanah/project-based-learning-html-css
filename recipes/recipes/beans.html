Beans

Description

Beans are a great source of protein and can be used in many dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of beans
  • 2 cups of water
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: spices or herbs

Steps

  1. Rinse the beans under cold water.
  2. In a pot, combine the beans and water.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1-2 hours until tender.
  4. Add salt and any optional spices or herbs to taste.
  5. Serve hot as a side dish or in salads.
